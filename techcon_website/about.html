About TechCon 2024

Our Mission

At TechCon 2024, our mission is to bring together the brightest minds in technology to share knowledge, foster innovation, and celebrate the achievements of the tech community in Africa.

What to Expect

Attendees can look forward to keynote speeches from industry leaders, hands-on workshops, panel discussions, and networking opportunities with professionals from various tech sectors.

Join Us!

Whether you're a developer, entrepreneur, student, or tech enthusiast, TechCon 2024 has something for everyone. Join us in shaping the future of technology in Africa!

Our History

TechCon was established in 2015 with the goal of creating a platform for African tech innovators to showcase their work and connect with like-minded individuals. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated tech events on the continent.

Past Highlights

Previous editions of TechCon have featured groundbreaking product launches, inspiring success stories, and collaborations that have led to significant advancements in the tech industry.

Past Speakers

Dr. Amina Karanja

AI Researcher at Nairobi Tech Labs, known for advancing machine learning applications in African agriculture. Her 2022 TechCon keynote inspired collaboration between farmers and tech innovators.

Michael Oduor

Founder of CodeHub Africa. Michael has empowered over 10,000 young developers through coding bootcamps and open-source initiatives. He spoke about the future of African software innovation.

Lydia Abebe

Cybersecurity Specialist passionate about digital safety and privacy. At TechCon 2023, she led an engaging session on protecting startups from cyber threats.
